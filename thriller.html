Thriller

Red Eye (2005)

Director: Wes Crave
Looking for a movie that is as exciting as Cillian Murphy’s cheekbones? Look no further! This action-packed thriller will possibly make you fear your next flight… and be amazed by another Rachel McAdams’ performance.

V for Vendetta (2005)

Director: James McTeigue
A classic story about oppression and rebellion enriched by a thrilling vision of a futuristic, totalitarian Britain and a unique relationship between the mysterious riot leader V (Hugo Weaving) and young Evey (Natalie Portman). Freedom forever!

Fight Club (1999)

Director: David Fincher
The first rule of fight club is you do not talk about fight club… so go and give it a try on your own. You won’t be disappointed.

The Devil's Advocate (1997)

Director: Taylor Hackford
Do you consider yourself a person of a strong moral backbone? See how a talented young lawyer (Keanu Reeves) deals with his own under the pressure of fame and money (or rather, the devil himself). Starring also Al Pacino and Charlize Theron.
