Documentary

The Last Dance (2020)

Director: Jason Hehir
Lauded as being one of the best basketball players of all-time, this documentary focuses on the career and impact of Michael Jordan. Focused on his last season in Chicago, it explores the Chicago Bulls that ruled the NBA for many years, as well as the dynamics of the team.

Our Planet (2019)

Director: Sophie Lanfear
Narrated by David Attenborough, this nature documentary series explores the wonders of nature around the globe. However, it places an important emphasis on the consequences of climate change on habitats. While serving as a reminder of the fascinating beauty of nature, it also sends a warning message regarding the impact of humans on the destruction of the environment.

The True Cost (2015)

Director: Andrew Morgan
Being the second most polluting industry in the world, the fashion industry and the exploitation that comes with it, is exposed throughout this documentary. Detailing the life of underpaid and exploited workers, and the environmental damage caused by this industry, it demonstrates the need for drastic change. It questions consumerism and makes viewers reflect on their choices and their impact.
