About Us


We are a small group of students who share a passion and an interest in film and TV series. With the constant release of new shows and new films, we understand it can sometimes become overwhelming when deciding what to watch. For this reason we decided to create this website where we share our favourite movies and series of different genres in order to make it easier for you to chose when you're at home trying to come to a decision.

We hope you enjoy the selection we have produced. We will continue to update the website with new recommendations, and if you want to send us a recommendation that you think should be on this website, feel free to do so!
