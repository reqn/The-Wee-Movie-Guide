Science Fiction

Sucker Punch (2011)

Director: Zack Snyder
This psychological science-fiction movie is worth seeing mostly for its hypnotising atmosphere, comic-like aesthetics and magnetising soundtrack.

The Fifth Element (1997)

Director: Luc Besson
A fine and original piece of science fiction. This movie is going to surprise you many times, even though the performance of Bruce Willis, Milla Jovovich and Gary Oldman is unsurprisingly good, as always.

Avatar (2009)

Director: James Cameron
This epic picture will take you to another planet. Watch as blue-skinned, majestic beings are confronted with humans and their cutting-edge technology in this FX-masterpiece and try to answer the question: what is the price of progress?

Lucy (2014)

Director: Luc Besson
A lot can be forgiven the science-fiction movies, even reviving the ten percent brain capacity myth. Especially, when it results in such a fast-paced and intriguing story.
