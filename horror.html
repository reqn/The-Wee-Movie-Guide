Horror

The Shining (1980)

Director: Stanley Kubrick
As with any Stanley Kubrick’s movie, having a lot of patience is necessary… But it’s worth it to see the cult classic, crowned by Jack Nicholson’s shiny smile.

Psycho (1960)

Director: Alfred Hitchcock
An intrinsic view on complicated mother-son relationship… with an unsettling twist. Make sure to watch it, so you are able to comprehend numerous references in the works of modern culture.

Get Out (2017)

Director: Jordan Peele
Meeting the parents of your second half might not be the easiest moment of your life – however, it is quite unlikely it will turn out like in this genre-bending movie. Or at least you should hope so.

Mother! (2017)

Director: Darren Aronofsky
You could call it an extremely unsettling horror… or a condensed image of the modern world. It’s not a classic genre representative – however, it may occur more horrifying than anything you’ve seen.
