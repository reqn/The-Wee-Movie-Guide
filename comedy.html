Comedy

Booksmart (2019)

Director: Olivia Wilde
This coming-of-age film documents two best friends who realise, as they are about to graduate high-school, that they have isolated themselves in their studies and should have gone out and enjoyed themselves more. In an effort to make the best of their last days in high school and make up for their wasted years, they decide to break all the rules.

Perfect Strangers (2017)

Director: Álex de la Iglesia
The Spanish adaptation of Italian film ‘Perfetti sconosciuti’, this film follows four couples who have known each other their whole lives and decide to play a game at a dinner party. This game consists of reading all the messages that they receive and putting their calls on loudspeaker throughout the night. This seemingly innocent game however quickly reveals many secrets.

Bridesmaids (2011)

Director: Paul Feig
Asked to be her best-friend’s maid of honour, Annie’s already tumultuous and messy life takes a turn for the worse as she tries to get everything together for the wedding, whilst simultaneously competing with the bride’s new friend Helen.

Crazy, Stupid, Love (2011)

Director: Glenn Ficarra, John Requa
After his wife asks for a divorce, Cal Weaver, who had what seemed to be a perfect life, with a great job and an ideal family, sees everything around him crumble. In an attempt to start his new life, he seeks help from a young, single friend.
